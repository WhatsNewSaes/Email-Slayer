Email Slayer

For generations, chaos raged across the lands. Seemingly unconquerable email client foes stirred from the abyss tormenting all who dared to write a line of code against their name. Many heroes rose up in attempt to tip balance between good and evil, however, there was no single power effective enough to slay these depraved, twisted creatures. Take heart, young warrior. Email Slayer is here to help you tame these ravenous beasts.
